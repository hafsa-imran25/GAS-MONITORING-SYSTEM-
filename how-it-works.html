How SmartGas Works

SmartGas uses a sensor attached to the cylinder to measure weight/pressure and sends updates to the cloud. The app or dashboard receives live updates and alerts you when action is required.

  • Sensor records weight & calculates percentage
  • Device sends data over Wi-Fi/Cellular, Bluetooth
  • Cloud stores and forwards realtime updates to your app
  • Intelligent alerts for leaks and low gas
